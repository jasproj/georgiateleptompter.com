How to Write for a Teleprompter

Write for the ear, not the page.

Short Sentences

Conversational language improves delivery. Whether you're addressing Atlanta business leaders at the Metro Atlanta Chamber, presenting at Savannah's historic plant tours, or speaking at the Augusta National Masters, your script should sound natural when read aloud.

Georgia-Specific Considerations

Georgia's diverse economy—from Atlanta's Fortune 500 headquarters to Savannah's thriving port and Augusta's tech corridor—means your script should connect with varied audiences. Keep sentences under 20 words for smooth delivery.
