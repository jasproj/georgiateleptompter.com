Pros & Cons Breakdown

✅ Professional Teleprompter — Pros

  • Operator manages script pacing in real-time
  • Larger text readable from natural speaking distance
  • Broadcast-quality glass maintains eye contact with camera
  • Backup equipment prevents show-stopping failures
  • You focus on delivery, not technology

❌ Cons

  • Higher cost per use
  • Requires advance booking

✅ DIY iPad — Pros

  • Low upfront cost
  • Available anytime
  • Fine for practice and casual content

❌ Cons

  • Small screen forces speakers closer to camera
  • No real-time operator adjustment
  • Eye contact often looks "off"
  • No backup if something fails
  • You manage tech while trying to perform

Choose Professional when:

  • Working on film/TV productions or studio shoots
  • Stakes are high (investors, media, executives)
  • Content will be broadcast or widely distributed
  • Event has zero tolerance for technical issues

Choose DIY when:

  • Recording casual YouTube/social content
  • Practicing speeches before the real event
  • Budget is the only constraint

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I just use a teleprompter app on my iPad?

Yes, apps like PromptSmart and Teleprompter Premium work for basic use. However, the small screen size limits readability beyond 3–5 feet, and you won't have real-time operator control during your presentation.

How much does professional teleprompter rental cost in Georgia?

Professional teleprompter rental in Georgia ranges from $600–$800 for a half day and $1,200–$1,500 for a full day, including an experienced operator who manages everything.

Is DIY teleprompter good enough for film and TV productions in Georgia?

For casual content, possibly. But Georgia's booming film industry — from Tyler Perry Studios to major network productions — demands broadcast-quality equipment and experienced operators. Professional results require professional gear.
