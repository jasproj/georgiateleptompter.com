Serving Georgia's Film Industry

Georgia has become one of the top destinations for film and television production in the United States, earning the nickname "Hollywood of the South." With major studios like Pinewood Atlanta Studios and Tyler Perry Studios, plus generous state tax incentives, productions of all sizes choose Georgia as their home.

Georgia Teleprompter is proud to serve this thriving industry, providing professional teleprompting services for feature films, television series, commercials, and more. Our operators understand the unique demands of film production and integrate seamlessly with existing production crews.

Our Services

We provide full-service teleprompting for live events, corporate productions, film and video shoots, and broadcast. Our experienced operators work with current-generation equipment to ensure flawless delivery every time.
